## CCPA/CPRA overview

The California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act emphasize consumer rights, opt-out mechanisms, data sensitivity, and governance over personal information.

## Core concepts

- Consumer rights to know, delete, and correct personal information
- Right to opt out of sale or sharing of personal information
- Sensitive personal information and heightened handling expectations
- Service provider and contractor obligations
- Risk-based governance for processing and retention
